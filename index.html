About Us

Bawarchi is a family-friendly restaurant serving delicious food since 2024. Our mission is to provide quality meals and a comfortable dining experience.Bawarchi is a famous Hyderabad restaurant, particularly celebrated for its authentic Hyderabadi biryani, both chicken and mutton. The original location is at RTC X roads and is known for its flavorful, spicy dishes like dum biryani, along with a variety of seafood and Mughlai options. While it is a popular dining spot, the original RTC X roads location has a busy, bustling atmosphere.
